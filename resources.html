Important LGBTQ Websites

Trevor Project

This organization provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ youth. Their website offers a wealth of resources, including a 24/7 helpline, chat, and text support.

GLAAD

GLAAD is dedicated to promoting LGBTQ acceptance and ensuring fair, accurate, and inclusive representation in the media. Their website is a hub for news, media resources, and advocacy efforts.

Human Rights Campaign

HRC is one of the largest civil rights organizations working to achieve equality for LGBTQ individuals. Their website provides information on legislation, advocacy campaigns, and resources for coming out and living openly.

PFLAG

PFLAG is the first and largest organization for LGBTQ people, their parents, families, and allies. Their website offers support, education, and advocacy resources.

It Gets Better Project

This project aims to uplift, empower, and connect LGBTQ youth around the globe. Their website features inspiring stories, videos, and resources.

Out Magazine

As one of the leading sources for LGBTQ news, fashion, entertainment, and lifestyle, Out Magazine's website is a go-to for staying informed and connected with LGBTQ culture.
